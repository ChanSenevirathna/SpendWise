Features

Built for real budgeting

Everything from the requirements spec, in one place

Multi-Wallet Tracking
Track Cash In Hand, bank accounts & more. Auto-calculated balances with wallet-to-wallet transfers.
Dual Allocation
Split every income across wallets AND categories, with validation ensuring all totals match.
Budget Categories
Allocate to Food, Travel, Savings & more. See remaining budgets and overspent alerts at a glance.
Visual Dashboard
Live charts for balance-by-wallet and spending-by-category, updating in real time.
Google Sign-In
Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with session restore and encrypted cloud sync.
Excel Reports
Generate expense, category-wise and wallet-wise reports for any date range.
Cloud Sync & Backup
Data syncs securely with automatic backup and manual restore, encrypted at rest.
Mobile-First
Responsive UI for Android, iOS and web — built to enter a transaction in under 2 minutes.
